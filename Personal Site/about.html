What Makes Ashleigh Tick

Family

My greatest accomplishment is my family. My husband of twenty years is my best friend, life partner, ride or die and my rock.

We have made a life complete with one girl, three boys, and two bougie dogs. Guided by the idea that it's not about what you tell your children, but how you show them how to live life, we have set out to raise our kids differently than we were raised. We're showing them to invest in themselves and invest relationships with their partners. We're showing them to see their children as the individuals they are and value their views about the world around them. One of my favorite times with them is at the dinner table, we have the most random conversations!

Travel

We have an amazing, huge world to explore! I take every opportunity to see all the world has to offer. Travel challenges us to question beliefs we have and understand more fully the context from which those came. While teaching us that as many differences we may have, we have many more similarities. I feel like each time you take yourself out of your comfort zone, you learn a little more about yourself. You learn to appreciate other cultures, other languages, other viewpoints; an that I believe is how we work to work through our differences, recognize our similarities and come together!

Fitness Enthusiast

I love fitness in every shape! From hiking to skiing, to paddleboarding, weightlifting, and everything in between.

In 2020, I became a certified yoga instructor.

In 2021 I caught the Peloton bug. I love the community Peloton offers to people from all walks of life, and all corners of the world coming together in a common goal to carve out a small bit of our busy day for ourselves.
